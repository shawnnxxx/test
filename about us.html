About IronFit

Built by athletes, for athletes. Our story of strength and dedication.

Our Story

IronFit Apparel was born in 2018 from a simple idea: create gym wear that actually works as hard as you do. Founded by competitive powerlifter Jake Reynolds, what started as a small garage operation has grown into a movement embraced by athletes worldwide.

From Garage to Global

Our journey began with a single sewing machine and a relentless drive to solve the problems we faced as athletes. Tired of shirts that ripped during deadlifts and shorts that couldn't handle squat sessions, we set out to engineer apparel that could withstand the most intense training.

Today, every IronFit product is still tested by our team of competitive athletes before it reaches you. We've grown, but our commitment to quality and performance remains unchanged.
