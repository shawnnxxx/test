Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take?

We typically process and ship orders within 1-2 business days. Shipping times vary based on your location:

  • USA: 3-5 business days
  • Canada: 5-7 business days
  • International: 7-14 business days

You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships.

What shipping methods do you offer?

We offer several shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping: Free on orders over $50 (USA only)
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days ($9.99)
  • International Shipping: Rates calculated at checkout

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once your order has shipped, you'll receive an email with your tracking number. You can track your package directly through our website by logging into your account or using the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy from the date of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached. To initiate a return:

  1. Log into your account and go to "Order History"
  2. Select the order and items you wish to return
  3. Print the prepaid return label
  4. Package your return and drop it off at any UPS location

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your return.

Do you offer exchanges?

Yes! We offer free size exchanges within 30 days of purchase. To request an exchange:

  1. Contact our customer service team at support@ironfit.com
  2. Provide your order number and the new size you need
  3. We'll ship your new item once we receive your return

Note: Exchanges are subject to availability. If your desired size is out of stock, we'll issue a refund instead.

Product Information

How do I choose the right size?

We provide detailed size charts for each product on its product page. For the best fit:

  • Measure your chest, waist, and hips
  • Compare your measurements to our size chart
  • Consider your preferred fit (slim, regular, or relaxed)

If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a looser fit or down for a more compressive fit.

How should I care for my IronFit apparel?

To maintain the quality and performance of your IronFit gear:

  • Machine wash cold with similar colors
  • Use mild detergent (avoid fabric softeners)
  • Hang dry or tumble dry low
  • Do not iron directly on prints or logos
  • Turn garments inside out before washing

Proper care will extend the life of your performance apparel.

Are your products sweat-wicking?

Yes! All IronFit apparel features advanced moisture-wicking technology that pulls sweat away from your skin to keep you dry and comfortable during intense workouts. Our fabrics are:

  • Quick-drying
  • Breathable
  • Odor-resistant
  • Stretch-recovery for full range of motion

Account & Payments

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is easy! Click the "Create Account" icon in the top navigation or click here to get started. You'll need to provide:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Password

With an account, you can track orders, save your shipping information, and access exclusive member benefits.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods:

  • Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay
  • Cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum)

All payments are processed securely through our encrypted checkout system.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We take security seriously and use industry-standard encryption to protect your payment information. Our checkout process is:

  • PCI-DSS compliant
  • SSL encrypted
  • Tokenized for additional security

We never store your full credit card information on our servers.
